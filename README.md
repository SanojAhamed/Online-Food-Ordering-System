# Online-Food-Ordering-System
Online Food Ordering System Includes the Entire Process of Ordering Food through a Digital Platform.

Deli, where great food meets convenience. Our online platform, launched in 2023 by designed by a dedicated team of second-year students from UWU. Offers a user-friendly website to explore our diverse menu, place orders, leave reviews, and provide feedback. Exclusive discounts and special offers can be enjoyed. Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we strive to create memorable dining experiences.
